Ethics & Privacy in AI

Why Ethics in AI matters

AI systems impact hiring, healthcare, justice, and more. Without transparency and accountability, they can reinforce discrimination, leak private data, or be misused.

Case Study: Should AI decide who gets hired?

Imagine a company uses an LLM to screen job applicants. Would you support this?

Responsible AI Checklist

  • Is the training data anonymized and secure?
  • Has the system been tested for bias?
  • Is there human oversight on decisions?
  • Are users informed about limitations?
  • Are results explainable and transparent?
